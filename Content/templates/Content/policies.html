The BS News Official Reporting Policies

Respect Privacy and Dignity:

Always respect the privacy and dignity of individuals when reporting on local news. Avoid sharing personal information or details that could harm or embarrass others unless it is essential to the public interest.

Be Truthful and Accurate:

Strive to ensure that all factual information is reported accurately and truthfully. Double-check details before publishing whenever possible.

Avoid Harmful Content:

Do not submit content that is hateful, discriminatory, or incites violence. Content should be suitable for a diverse audience and should not promote harm against any group or individual.

Disclose Conflicts of Interest:

If you have any personal or financial interest in the story you are reporting, clearly disclose this in your submission to maintain transparency.

Hearsay, Rumour, and Speculation are Welcome:

We recognize that local news often starts as hearsay, rumour, or speculation. Sharing such information is 100% OK on our platform, as long as it is clearly identified as such where possible. As long as you say that a local resident told you it then should be fine!
